What We Showcase

DISCIPLINES

Fine Art

14 Artists

Fine art, in Vibrations, is the art of creating illustrations and paintings that push the boundaries of traditional mediums. Our Fine Arts students explore their creativity at EVCC and create unique pieces that you won't find in everyday life.

Photography

14 Artists

The art and science of capturing light to create an image, whether through a digital sensor or chemical film. Our Photography Students showcase the power and beauty behind each picture taken, showing many forms of perspective.

Ceramics

7 Artists

The art of creating objects from clay, which are then fired at high temperatures to create durable and beautiful pieces. Our Ceramics Students explore the tactile nature of clay and the transformative power of the firing process.

Graphic Design

9 Artists

The art of creating visual content to communicate messages effectively. Our Graphic Design Students explore the intersection of aesthetics and functionality in their work.

Writing

5 Artists

The art of creating written content to communicate messages effectively. Our Writing Students explore the power of language and storytelling in their work.
