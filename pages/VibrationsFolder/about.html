ABOUT
VIBRATIONS

"Art should make you feel something you don't have a name for yet."

VIBRATIONS; a curated artist collective. We're Booklab - a small, tight-knit group responisble for this years Vibrations Magazine! Much like our publication, we're quite a vibrant group. We are diligent, dedicated, and passionate; Both in and out of class. We hope you enjoy our hard work, and more importantly: the hard work of the artists.

1963

Founded in 1963, VIBRATIONS is a collective of independent artists across 5 disciplines, united by a shared vision to create bold, unconventional art that moves people emotionally and culturally.

Our Manifesto

WHY WE EXIST

Evcc Vibrations exists to create art that moves people emotionally, culturally, and creatively. We believe art is more than expression — it is energy, storytelling, and connection that can inspire new perspectives and bring communities together.

Our purpose is to produce meaningful creative experiences that amplify authentic voices, challenge convention, and leave a lasting impact through visual, experiential art.

We exist to transform ideas into living art that people can feel, remember, and carry with them long after the moment ends.

As we continue to grow through visual art, production, and immersive experiences, we transform raw ideas into vibrations that carry meaning, energy, and story. Every project we create is rooted in authenticity, collaboration, and the desire to leave a lasting emotional impact.

Because art is more than entertainment.

It is Connection.

It is Movement.

It is Vibrations.

What We Stand For

OUR VALUES

01

Community & Connection

Art is meant to bring people together. We value collaboration, shared experiences, and building spaces where people feel seen, inspired, and connected.

02

Creative Innovation

We believe creativity should constantly evolve. We push boundaries, experiment fearlessly, and embrace new ways to bring ideas to life.

03

Authentic Expression

We believe in creating art that is genuine and true to the artist's vision. We value honesty, integrity, and the courage to express oneself authentically.

04

Freedom Without Limits

We encourage bold thinking, artistic freedom, and the courage to create beyond expectations, trends, or convention.
